Boxing is a combat sport where two athletes fight using their fists, typically wearing gloves. The goal is to land punches on the opponent while avoiding being hit. It requires speed, strength, agility, and strategy. Boxing matches usually consist of multiple rounds, and a fighter wins by knockout (KO), technical knockout (TKO), or by scoring more points than the opponent over the course of the match. It's both a physical and mental challenge!
